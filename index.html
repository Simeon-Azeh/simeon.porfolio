Sidec

Ed-Tech

Sidec

Sidec is a platform that allows users to find questions, book tutors, and take practice exams. In summary, Sidec is a web app that allows students to practice and know how prepared they are for a National Exam. I am pleased to have worked on the front-end

About and my contribution to Sidec

As the frontend developer of Sidec, I bring to life a vision that goes beyond pixels and code. Our platform is a dynamic canvas where education meets innovation. From personalized mock exams to real-time syllabus courses, every click is a step toward empowering students across Africa.


Key Features

  • A user-friendly interface that empowers students to immerse themselves in learning.
  • Mock Exams: Simulating real national exams with a user-friendly interface.
  • Real-Time Syllabus Courses:** A personalized learning journey aligned with high school curriculums.
  • Graduates can effortlessly host courses and connect with students.
  • Bridging gaps with offline versions for limited internet access.

Sidec isn't just about functionality; it's about an experience. The design ensures smooth navigation, making learning engaging and intuitive, and as a frontend developer, I am proud to have been the lead frontend developer in this initiative
